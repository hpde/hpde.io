Observation data of the equatorial lower troposphere taken by the boundary layer radar (BLR)-1 at Serpong (CSV format)

ResourceID
spase://IUGONET/NumericalData/RISH/misc/SRP/BLradar_1/blr_1_srp_csv

Description

The 10-minute average observation data in the equatorial lower troposhpere (0.3-6.0 km) taken by the boundary layer radar (BLR)-1 at Serpong, one of the south-west suburbs of Jakarta, Indonesia (6.36S, 106.65E, 50m MSL). The observation data are stored in the CSV (Comma Separated Values) file named (year)(month)(day).(variable).csv. The variable abbreviations are uwnd, vwnd, wwnd, pwr1, pwr2, pwr3, wdt1, wdt2, and wdt3, which mean zonal, meridional and vertical wind velocities, echo power and spectral width for beam 1-3, respectively. The azimuth and zenith angles of beam 1, 2 and 3 are (0, 0), (0, 15), and (90, 15), respectively, in unit of degree. The numbers of the first line of each file represent altitudes, and the second and below are observational local date and time (year/month/day hour:minute) which corresponds to the center time of averaging period and data at each altitude. The value of 999 means missing data.
