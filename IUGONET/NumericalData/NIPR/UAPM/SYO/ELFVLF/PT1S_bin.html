ELF/VLF electromagnetic wave emission data with 1sec resolution at Syowa Station, Antarctica.

ResourceID
spase://IUGONET/NumericalData/NIPR/UAPM/SYO/ELFVLF/PT1S_bin

Description

Natural electromagnetic wave emissions at 9 fixed ELF/VLF frequency bands with 1sec resolution at Syowa Station, Antarctica. The data is originally measured at 0.05sec intervals with the 9-channel ELF/VLF receiver and stored together with the magnetic field from the fluxgate magnetometer, CNA from the broad-beam riometer, and the magnetic field from the induction magnetometer.

Acknowledgement
Please contact the Principal Investigator, Dr. Hisao Yamagishi (yamagisi [at] uap.nipr.ac.jp), National Institute of Polar Research (NIPR) before using the data at any publications and/or presentations. We welcome collaborative research and strongly recommend users to contact us early in order to ensure that the data are suitable for their research. When the data are used at any publications and/or presentations, we would like to ask the users to acknowledge NIPR and send us one copy of the reprints.
