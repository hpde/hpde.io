Global distribution of dry air temperature at 15 km derived from the COSMIC full spectrum inversion (FSI) data (PNG)

ResourceID
spase://IUGONET/DisplayData/RISH/COSMIC/COSMIC_FORMOSAT_3/IGOR/cosmic_formosat_3_igor_fsi_temperature_2dplot_png

Description

Global distribution of dry air temperature at 15 km calculated by applying full spectrum inversion (FSI) [Jensen et al., 2003] method to COSMIC GPS-RO profiles. The temperature was averaged in a box with +/-5.0 degrees in geographical longitude and latitude and +/-1.0 km height. The horizontal and vertical axes are geographic longitude and latitude, respectively. The color bar is a dry air temperature between -80 and -40 degree C. File name of the height-latitude plot is composed of YYYYMMDD_temperature_2dmap_15.0000km.png. YYYY, MM, and DD indicate year, month, and day, respectively.
