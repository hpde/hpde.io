Description

The observation data taken by the visible all-sky camera at Shigaraki MU Observatory in Shiga Prefecture, Japan (34.86N, 136.11E, 385m MSL), which has been operated on November 4, 2010. File names of observation data are composed of date and time: (year)(month)(day)_(hour)(minute).jpg. The time interval of each image is 1 minutes. Local time (UT + 09:00) is used for time data.
