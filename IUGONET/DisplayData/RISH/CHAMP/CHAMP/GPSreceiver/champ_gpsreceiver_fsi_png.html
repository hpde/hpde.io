CHAMP full spectrum inversion (FSI) data (PNG)

ResourceID
spase://IUGONET/DisplayData/RISH/CHAMP/CHAMP/GPSreceiver/champ_gpsreceiver_fsi_png

Description

The height-latitude plot of zonal-mean dry air temperature calculated by applying full spectrum inversion (FSI) [Jensen et al., 2003] method to COSMIC GPS-RO profiles in an altitude from ground level up to 30 km. The color bar is zonal-mean dry air temperature in a range from -100 to 20 degree C. File name of the height-latitude plot is composed of YYYYMMDD_temperature_profile.png. YYYY, MM, and DD indicate year, month, and day, respectively.
