Standard radio wave phase-amplitude variation data of Pontianak station (Quick Look)

ResourceID
spase://IUGONET/DisplayData/TohokuU/radio_obs/ptk/lf/avon_lfrto_ptk_display

Description

Amplitude and phase time series of low frequency standard radio transmitter signals received at the Pontianak station (Indonesia) of Tohoku University. Variations in the phase and amplitude reflect changes of the ionization state in the lower ionosphere which is caused by energetic electron precipitation from inner magnetosphere (radiation belt), solar x-rays, lightning dischanges, and so on.
