OMTI Tilting Photometer (TILT2) at Rikubetsu, Japan

ResourceID
spase://IUGONET/DisplayData/ISEE/OMTI/RIK/TILT/TILT2

Description

Optical Mesosphere Thermosphere Imagers (OMTIs) Tilting Photometer -2 (TILT2) Data. The TILT2 measures OH and 630.0nm airglow and aurora intensities in the mesopause region at 80-100km altitudes and in the thermosphere at 200-300km altitudes at 5 directions (+-74.88, +-37.44, 0deg) in the nort-south meridian. For more information, see http://stdb2.isee.nagoya-u.ac.jp/omti/. Digital data are available upon request to PI.
