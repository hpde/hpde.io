Realtime quicklook images taken by the all-sky imager at Kiruna, Sweden

ResourceID
spase://IUGONET/DisplayData/NIPR/EISCAT/KIR/AWI/AWI_realtime

Description

Realtime quicklook images taken by the all-sky imager (body: WATEC WAT-120N+, lens: FUJINON YV2.2×1.4A-SA2) with some filters at Kiruna, Sweden. The all images are originally recorded in the JPEG format with a temporal resolution of 1s, 1s, 0.5s, and 4s for color, 558nm, 670nm, and 428nm, respectively.
