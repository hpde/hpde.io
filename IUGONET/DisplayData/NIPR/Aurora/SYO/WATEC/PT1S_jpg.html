All-sky auroral image taken by the white-light all-sky camera at Syowa Station, Antarctica.

Description

Auroral all-sky images taken by the white-light all-sky CCD camera (body: WATEC WAT-120N+, lens: FUJINON YV2.2×1.4A-SA2) at Syowa Station, Antarctica. All images are recorded in the JPEG format with a temporal resolution of ~1s. Three types of summary plot are available from the project web site: 1-min all-sky images in PDF, 1-night keogram in PNG, and 1-night movie in mpeg. If you need the original 1-sec resolution data for the purpose of scientific researches, please contact Dr. Akira Kadokura (kadokura [at] nipr.ac.jp).
