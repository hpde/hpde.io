STIX QL Light Curves

ResourceID
spase://ESA/NumericalData/SolarOrbiter/STIX/QuickLook/QL_LightCurve/PT4S

Description

QL light curves contain time series of 4-s-integrated, detector-summed counts in five energy bands. The curves represent compressed counts without lifetime correction. For large flares, an attenuator is automatically inserted reducing counts at low energies. The data product is intend to be used for planing purposes; for science publications please use STIX science data products.
