SOHO Energetic and Relativistic Nuclei and Electron (ERNE) Experiment Heavy Ion Hourly Data in CDF Format

(2023). SOHO Energetic and Relativistic Nuclei and Electron (ERNE) Experiment Heavy Ion Hourly Data in CDF Format [Data set]. NASA Space Physics Data Facility. https://doi.org/10.48322/js86-0v08 (CiteAs link). Accessed on .

ResourceID
spase://ESA/NumericalData/SOHO/ERNE/HeavyIon/CDF/PT1H

Description

The data include ERNE hourly C, N, O, Ne, Mg, Si, C-O, Si-Ar, and Fe-Ni count rates and fluxes in 10 energy ranges (2.7-200 MeV/n), as well as accumulation time and status bit. This CDF dataset was converted from the original ASCII dataset at the SOHO Archive https://soho.nascom.nasa.gov/data/archive.html. Description of the ERNE instrument and scientific scope can be found on the ERNE home page https://srl.utu.fi/projects/erne/ and on the SOHO home page https://sohowww.nascom.nasa.gov/. ERNE Instrument Publication: Torsti, J., Valtonen, E., Lumme, M. et al. Energetic particle experiment ERNE, Sol Phys 162, 505–531 (1995), https://doi.org/10.1007/BF00733438.
