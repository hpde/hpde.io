Description

Data products of the Cluster Plasma Electron
and Current Experiment (PEACE) instruments which have been produced or the Cluster Active Archive. The Plasma Electron And Current Experiment (PEACE) measurements
of the thermal electron plasma population enable characterisation of the
local plasma – its density, bulk flow, electron temperature – and electron
pitch angle data reveal whether the local region is magnetically connected
to regions of electron acceleration or lies between magnetic mirrors.
PEACE electron data reveals whether electron pitch angle or energy scat-
tering processes are active, for example as wave-particle interactions oc-
