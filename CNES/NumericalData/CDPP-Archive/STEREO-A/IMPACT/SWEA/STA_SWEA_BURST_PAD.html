STEREO-A IMPACT SWEA Level-2 Burst electron pitch-angle distributions in phase space density

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-Archive/STEREO-A/IMPACT/SWEA/STA_SWEA_BURST_PAD

Description

This data set contains the electron pitch angle distributions (from 0° to 180°) as a function of energy at the BURST cadence (2 s).
The pitch-angles are calculated in the plasma frame based on ion velocity from the PLASTIC instrument (if data is available, according to a flag given in the dataset).
The data is provided in units of phase space density. The reference frame is related to the instrument frame.
