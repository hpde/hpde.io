Double Star 2 HID Prime Parameters

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-Archive/DoubleStar2/HID/DSTAR_PP_T2_HID

Description

The Double Star Hot Ion Detector (HID) instruments are located on both the equatorial and polar satellites. They measure measure alpha-particles in the energy range 12 – 350 MeV in 4 channels (12-50 MeV, 50-130 MeV, 130-220 MeV, 220-350 MeV, and some heavier ions with larger atomic mass numbers from Li to Fe (mass 26) in 6 channels.

This data set contains Double Star 2 HID prime parameters at spin resolution. These parameters include instrument status and ion fluxes (He, Li, Be, B, C, N, O, F, Mg, Ar, Fe).
