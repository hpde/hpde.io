Double Star 2 NUADU Summary Parameters

Description

The NUADU (NeUtral Atom Detector Unit) instrument is an ENA-sensitive imager that covers 4-π solid angle with an angular resolution of 16 × 128 = 2048 pixels.

This data set conatins Double Star 2 NUADU summary parameters at 1 min including the instrument status and omni-directional ENA fluxes in 4 energy bands.
