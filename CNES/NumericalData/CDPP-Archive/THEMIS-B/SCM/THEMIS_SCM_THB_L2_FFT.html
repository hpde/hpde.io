Description

This dataset contains the calibrated power spectral density (computed on board) of electric and magnetic fields from the Electric Field Instrument (EFI) and the Search Coil Magnetometer (SCM) on board THEMIS-B.

Le Contel the co-investigator (CoI) of the SCM instrument to consult on the appropriate use of instrument data.
