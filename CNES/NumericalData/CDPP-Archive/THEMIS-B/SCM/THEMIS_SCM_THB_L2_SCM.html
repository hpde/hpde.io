THEMIS-B SCM (Search Coil Magnetometer) calibrated magnetic field in FAST SURVEY, PARTICLE BURST and WAVE BURST modes.

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-Archive/THEMIS-B/SCM/THEMIS_SCM_THB_L2_SCM

Description

This dataset contains calibrated THEMIS-B SCM (Search Coil Magnetometer) magnetic field in FAST SURVEY, PARTICLE BURST and WAVE BURST modes.

The time resolutions are:

FAST SURVEY (SCF): 1/8 s.

PARTICLE BURST (SCP): 1/128 s (only short bursts of data).

WAVE BURST (SCW): 1/8192 s (only short bursts of data).
