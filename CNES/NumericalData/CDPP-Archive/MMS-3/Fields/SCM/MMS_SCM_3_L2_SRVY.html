MMS-3 SCM Search Coil Magnetometer Level-2 Survey Waveform (CDF)

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-Archive/MMS-3/Fields/SCM/MMS_SCM_3_L2_SRVY

Description

This data sets contains MMS-3 SCM (search coil magnetometer) calibrated AC magnetic field waveforms in the survey mode and GSE frame.
These waveform data are sampled at (survey scsrvy=32 samples/s, brst scb=8192 samples/s, schb=16384 samples/s). They are high-pass filtered above (scsrvy = 0.5 Hz, scb = 1 Hz, schb = 32 Hz) but not low-pass filtered.
