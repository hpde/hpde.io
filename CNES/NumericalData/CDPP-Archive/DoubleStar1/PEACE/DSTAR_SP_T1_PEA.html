Double Star 1 PEACE Summary Parameters

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-Archive/DoubleStar1/PEACE/DSTAR_SP_T1_PEA

Description

The Plasma Electron and Current Experiment (PEACE) provides the electron velocity distribution function at the satellite in three dimensions with good time, energy, and angular resolution for electron energies up to ~25 keV.

This data set contains Double Star 1 PEACE summary parameters at 1 min resolution including the instrument status, the electron density, electron bulk velocity, electron temperature, and electron heat flux.
