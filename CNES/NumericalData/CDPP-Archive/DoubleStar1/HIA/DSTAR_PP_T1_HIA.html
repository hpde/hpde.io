Description

The Double Star 1 Hot Ion Analyzer (HIA) is an ion energy-spectrometry experiment, capable of obtaining full three-dimensional ion distributions with good angular and time resolution (one spacecraft spin).

The HIA prime parameter data set contains various parameters at spin resolution. These parameters include universal time, the instrument status, the ion density, ion bulk velocity, and ion temperature.
