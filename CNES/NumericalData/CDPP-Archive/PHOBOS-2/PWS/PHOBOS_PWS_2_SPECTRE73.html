PHOBOS-2 PWS Electric field spectra, smoothed data

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-Archive/PHOBOS-2/PWS/PHOBOS_PWS_2_SPECTRE73

Description

This dataset contains E-field measurements from the PWS instrument of the APV-F experiment onboard PHOBOS-2:
average of the low frequency fluctuations from 0.2 to 10 Hz (first value).
smoothed spectrum obtained by processing r.m.s. outputs from 24 filters between 5 Hz and 150 kHz(72 last values).
