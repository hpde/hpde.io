Parameter #4
Name
Level of the transmission signal
Description

Takes one of the following values: 1.000 full level (156 mV RMS in MIP mode, 30 V RMS in LDL mode) - 0.500 nominal level (78 mV RMS in MIP mode, 15 V RMS in LDL mode) - 0.250 low level (39 mV RMS in MIP mode, 7.5 V RMS in LDL mode) - 0.125 minimum level (19.5 mV RMS in MIP mode, 3.75 V RMS in LDL mode)
