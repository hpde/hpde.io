BepiColombo - MPO - SIXS - cruise - SIXS-P sensor - derived

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-AMDA/BepiColombo/MPO/SIXS/bepi-sixs-cruise.l3

Description

DERIVED 2-minute-averaged energetic particle flux data obtained by the SIXS-P sensor aboard the BepiColombo MPO s/c.
All flux values are GROUND CALIBRATED data in units of 1/(cm^2 sr s MeV) and averaged to 2-Minute-Mean values.
The flux is given in four viewing directions, indicated by prefixes Side0_, Side1_, Side2_, and Side3_ in the column names.
Noisy periods and periods with significant packet loss between the instrument and back-end electronics were removed.
These data were calculated using the results of the so-called bow-tie analysis, which means that the fluxes are given at an effective
energy of the channel. This approximation works well only when the energy spectrum of the particles is a decreasing function of energy.

  For a full description of the data, please refer to "A description of the BepiColombo/SIXS-P cruise phase data product", 
  available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.13692883
  
  Effective energy (MeV) - Electron channels - Side 0 : 0.0714 - 0.106 - 0.169 - 0.278 - 0.918 - 2.12 - 7.01

  Effective energy (MeV) - Electron channels - Side 1-4 : 0.0726 - 0.107 - 0.168 - 0.275 - 0.918 - 2.22 - 6.46

  Effective energy (MeV) - Proton channels - Side 0 : 1.09 - 1.19 - 1.51 - 2.26 - 3.94 - 8.02 - 14.3 - 25.4 - 49.8

  Effective energy (MeV) - Proton channels - Side 1-4 : 1.12 - 1.22 - 1.53 - 2.28 - 3.94 - 8.02 - 14.5 - 25.1 - 49.8
 
  Effective energy (MeV) - Proton channels as electron ones - Side 0 : 0.468 - 0.824 - 2.22
  
  Effective energy (MeV) - Proton channels as electron ones - Side 1-4 : 0.459 - 0.854 - 2.47
