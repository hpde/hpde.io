Geotail - MGF - editor B

ResourceID
spase://CNES/NumericalData/CDPP-AMDA/GEOTAIL/MGF/gtl-mgf-edb

Description

3-second Editor-B magnetic field data from the Geotail spacecraft.

        As of February 1 2008 the Bz component of the magnetic field has been corrected in the data set.

        The Geotail satellite has two editors onboard: 
        
        The Editor-A data are only acquired with the real-time operation in Usuda Deep Space Center (UDSC) or Uchinoura Space Center (USC), Japan, while the Editor-B data are continuously recorded in the onboard tape recorders for 24 hours and are dumped to the NASA JPL Deep Space Network (DSN) stations.
