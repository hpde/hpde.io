ResourceID

Description

The Summary Parameter Plots are plots of a subset of the Summary Parameters, one minute resolution, 6 hours per page, 4 pages for all the parameters plotted. There are thus 16 pages per day. These plots are in the form of PostScript files, produced by the German Data Centre.
