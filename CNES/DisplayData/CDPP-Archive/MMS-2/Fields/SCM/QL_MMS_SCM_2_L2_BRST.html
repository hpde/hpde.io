MMS-2 SCM L2 Burst Quicklooks (PNG)

ResourceID
spase://CNES/DisplayData/CDPP-Archive/MMS-2/Fields/SCM/QL_MMS_SCM_2_L2_BRST

Description

MMS-2 SCM burst quicklooks [1-4096Hz] shows from top to bottom : color coded data quality, waveforms (nT), power spectral densities (nT^2/Hz), integrated power (nT^2), time-averaged (over the burst time period) Fourier spectra (nT^2/Hz) with SCM ground noise level (in black) for reference.

All data are plotted in GSE.
