Demeter QuickLooks (Postscript)

ResourceID
spase://CNES/DisplayData/CDPP-Archive/DEMETER/Quicklooks/DMT_QL

Description

DEMETER Quicklooks present data of all the science experiments.
It represents half-orbit data with a low time resolution.

  • Date and orbit number,
  • Mode frame (Survey or Burst),
  • ICE experiment : 3 plots (spectra and waveforms),
  • IMSC experiment : 1 plot (spectra),
  • RNF neural network : 1 plot,
  • IDP experiment : 1 plot (counters),
  • IAP experiment : 2 plots (APR and ADV detectors),
  • ISL experiment : 2 plots (spectra and curves),
  • Earthquake information : 1 plot,
  • Orbit parameters : 1 plot.

Acknowledgement
The DEMETER experiments are described in the special issue of Planetary and Space Science published in April 2006. We recommend the data users to contact the PI of the corresponding experiment early in their study. An example of acknowledgments could be: This work is based on observations with ICE, IMSC, RNF, IAP, IDP and ISL instruments embarked on the satellite DEMETER launched by CNES (Centre National d'Etudes Spatiales). The author thanks J.J. Berthelier, M. Parrot, J.A. Sauvaud, J.P. Lebreton and J.L. Pinçon, PI's of these instruments for the use of the data, and CDPP (Centre des Données de la Physique des Plasmas) for the provision of these data.
