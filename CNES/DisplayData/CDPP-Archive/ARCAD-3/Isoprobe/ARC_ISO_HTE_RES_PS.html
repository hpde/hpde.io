ARCAD3 ISOPROBE high resolution density waveforms (PostScripts)

ResourceID
spase://CNES/DisplayData/CDPP-Archive/ARCAD-3/Isoprobe/ARC_ISO_HTE_RES_PS

Description

This data set contains ARCAD-3 Isoprobe plots of waveform (tracking frequency of the extremum).
The high resolution data correspond to a frequency tracking of the resonance or an anti-resonance of the mutual impedance response. As the resonance frequency is related to the electron density and the anti-resonance frequency to the Debye length, these data (used as waveform data) allow studies of density and/or temperature fluctuations.
The extremum trackings, one per probe, are available in the Self-Oscillating mode, Maximum and Minimum search modes. The different combinations are : AUT-AUT, MAX-MAX, MAX-MIN and MIN-MIN. The number of samples and the time resolution depend on the type of telemetry : 256 samples with a resolution of 50 ms in TMS120, 200 samples with a resolution of 20 ms in TMS30, 1680 samples with a resolution of 2.5 ms in TMS4 and 19672 samples with a resolution of 0.2 ms in TMF.
