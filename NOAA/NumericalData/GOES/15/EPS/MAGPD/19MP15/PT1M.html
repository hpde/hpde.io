Description

Direct link to the GOES 15 Magnetospheric Proton Detector (MAGPD) data in CSV format as well as the energetic particle data from other GOES spacecraft. The data are sorted by year, month, spacecraft, and file type. The file names indicates the data type, spacecraft number, year, month, and resolution. For example, g15_magpd_19me15_1m_20110101_20110131.csv, indicates GOES 15 magnetometer data at 1 min time resolution from 2011-01-01 to 2011-01-31.
