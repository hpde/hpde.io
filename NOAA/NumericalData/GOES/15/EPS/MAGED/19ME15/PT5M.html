GOES 15 Magnetospheric Electron Detector (MAGED) data with average flux of 40 keV at 5 min Time Resolution in Spacecraft Coordinates

ResourceID
spase://NOAA/NumericalData/GOES/15/EPS/MAGED/19ME15/PT5M

Description

The GOES 15 Magnetospheric Electron Detector (MAGED) are essentially the same as the Energetic Particle Sensor (EPS) on GOES 8-12. For more information, please see Onsager et al. 1996, Operational uses of the GOES energetic particle detectors, in GOES 8 and Beyond, Proc. SPIE, Vol. 2812, edited by E. R. Washwell, pp. 281-290, Bellingham, WA. These data file contain mainly information on the electron count rates and fluxes from 5 energy channels from 40 to 475 keV for all 9 telescopes. Data files contain long descriptive header.

Direct access to the GOES 15 Magnetospheric Electron Detector (MAGED) data
URL
https://satdat.ngdc.noaa.gov/sem/goes/data/avg/
Description

Direct link to the GOES 15 Magnetospheric Electron Detector (MAGED) data in CSV format as well as the energetic particle data from other GOES spacecraft. The data are sorted by year, month, spacecraft, and file type. The file names indicates the data type, spacecraft number, year, month, and resolution. For example, g15_maged_19me15_5m_20110101_20110131.csv, indicates GOES 15 magnetometer data at 5 min time resolution from 2011-01-01 to 2011-01-31.
