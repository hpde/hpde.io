Description

Direct link to the GOES 12 High Energy Proton and Alpha Detector (HEPAD) data in CSV format as well as the energetic particle data from other GOES spacecraft. The data are sorted by year, month, spacecraft, and file type. The file names indicates the data type, spacecraft number, year, month, and resolution. For example, g12_hepad_5m_20100401_20100430.csv, indicates GOES 12 magnetometer data at 5 min time resolution from 2010-04-01 to 2010-04-30.
