Major SEP events observed by GOES.

ResourceID
spase://NOAA/Catalog/GOES/Major_SEP_Events

Description

Catalog includes major SEP events observed by GOES.
During a major SEP event particle intensity in the E>10 MeV proton channel exceeds 10 particles/(cm^2 s sr).
GOES >10 MeV fluxes are not available at OMNIWeb after 2020 March 04. After that date, we use unofficial NOAA GOES >10 MeV fluxes obtained from the ISWA HAPI server (https://iswa.gsfc.nasa.gov/IswaSystemWebApp/hapi/; id=goesp_part_flux_P5M; name=P10).
