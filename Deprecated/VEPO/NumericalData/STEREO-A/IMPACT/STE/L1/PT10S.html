STEREO-A IMPACT/STE 10-s Level 1 Electron Spectra

ResourceID
spase://VEPO/NumericalData/STEREO-A/IMPACT/STE/L1/PT10S

Description

The IMPACT Suprathermal Electron (STE) Telescope data
consist of 10-sec resolution electron counts from each of 8 sensors in each
of 32 energy bands that span 1.9 keV to 100 keV. Only four sensors provide
unsaturated data that are scientifically usable.
