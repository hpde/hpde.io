CSSWE REPTile Level 1 6-s Counts and Position

ResourceID
spase://VSPO/NumericalData/CubeSat/CSSWE/REPTile/L1/Counts/PT6S

Description

This data set contains Level 1 corrected count rates from the Relativistic Electron and Proton Telescope integrated little experiment (REPTile)on board the Colorado Student Space Weather Experiment (CSSWE) CubeSat. Electron counts are provided for 0.5-1.7 MeV, 1.7-3.3 MeV, and >3.3 MeV. Proton counts are provided for 9-18 MeV, 19-30 MeV, and 30-40 MeV. All counts, in units of number per 6 seconds, have been corrected for transmission and temperature noise. L is calculated using the IGRF magnetic field model. Geographic latitude, longitude and altitude (km) are provided.
