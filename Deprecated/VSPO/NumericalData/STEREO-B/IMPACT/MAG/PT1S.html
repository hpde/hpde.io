STEREO-B IMPACT/MAG 1-Hz Magnetic Field Parameters

ResourceID
spase://VSPO/NumericalData/STEREO-B/IMPACT/MAG/PT1S

Description

This data product contains 1-s overlapped averages of solar wind
magnetic field data taken by the IMPACT/Magnetometer on STEREO-B. The interface
allows selection of STEREO A or STEREO B data and of vector quantities in RTN or
spacecraft coordinates. It also allows selection of either of two parameter sets:
[Bx, By, Bz, |B|] or [RBx, RBy, RBz, |B|, cone and clock angles]. Further, the
interface enables easy migration between plot vs. ASCII data presentation options
and among the three available resolutions (1, 8, 32 Hz).
