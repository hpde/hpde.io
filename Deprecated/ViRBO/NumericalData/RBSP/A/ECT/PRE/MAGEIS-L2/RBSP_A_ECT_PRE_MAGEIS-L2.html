FESA: Spin-Averaged Differential Electron Flux ~20-4000 keV

Caveats
Spin-Averaged Differential Electron Flux (FESA). These data are obtained by averaging the sectored fluxes over the data accumulation time (a spin-set, nominally equal to 1 spin). Thus, it is not an omni-directional flux, but rather a spin-set-averaged flux. Dimension 1 is for the 25 magEIS energy channels. The energy channel centroids are specified in FESA_Energy. The channel widths are specified in FESA_Energy_Widths.

FPSA: Spin-Averaged Differential Proton Flux ~50-1500 keV

Caveats
Spin-Averaged Differential Proton Flux (FPSA). These data are obtained by averaging the sectored fluxes over the data accumulation time (a spin-set, nominally equal to 1 spin). Thus, it is not an omni-directional flux, but rather a spin-set-averaged flux. Dimension 1 is for the 31 magEIS energy channels. If less than 31 channels are used the remaining channel dimensions are set to fill values. The energy channel centroids are specified in FPSA_Energy. The channel widths are specified in FPSA_Energy_Widths.
