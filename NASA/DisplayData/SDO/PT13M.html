SDO Browse Images and Movies

ResourceID

Description

Solar movies and imagery from each of the 10 AIA
(Atmospheric Imaging Assembly) wavelengths, plus HMI (Helioseismic
and Magnetic Imager) magnetograms, intensitygrams and dopplergrams,
plus certain multi-wavelength and multi-sensor composites are
offered. Timespan and temporal and spatial resolution are user-
specified, subject to a 400 images per query limit.
