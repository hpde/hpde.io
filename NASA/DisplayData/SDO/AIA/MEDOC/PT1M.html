Description

These data consist of SDO AIA solar images at each of nine wavelengths (94 Å to 335 Å at 7 wavelengths, plus one each at 1600 Å and 1700 Å) at 1-min resolution. The interface lets the user specify a display of images at any combination of the nine available wavelengths and at any of nine time resolutions from one image per minute to one image per day. MEDOC is a facility hosted at the Institut d'Astrophysique Spatiale, IAS, a mixed entity from Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique, CNRS, and l'Université Paris-Sud, in collaboration with the Centre National d'Etudes Spatiales, CNES.
