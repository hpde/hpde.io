Big Events From Far Behind The Limb From Earth View With AIA Tri-Color Movies (Running Ratio)

ResourceID
spase://NASA/Catalog/STEREO/SECCHI/EUVI/BigEvents

Description

AIA movies of deeply occulted eruptions. Although they may not be useful for quantitative studies, these movies make it easy for us to find changes over time. The Javascript version gives us a wide range of control (such as changing speed, swinging, limiting the range of frames, etc.) The movies primarily consist of coronal images.
