RHESSI Flare Image Archive

ResourceID
spase://NASA/Catalog/NASA/RHESSI/Flare_Image_Archive

Description

The RHESSI Image Archive provides science-ready images for all RHESSI flares that meet time and flux thresholds. Images and related products were generated for >75,000 RHESSI flares. For each flare that meets the threshold, high quality images have been generated for an optimized grid of times and energies customized for that flare, using six of the RHESSI image reconstruction algorithms.
