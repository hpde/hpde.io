Ulysses ICME Event List

R. W. Ebert, D. J. McComas, H. A. Elliott, R. J. Forsyth, J. T. Gosling (2009). Ulysses ICME Event List [Data set]. Journal of Geophysical Research: Space Physics, Volume114, Issue A1,January 2009.

ResourceID
spase://NASA/Catalog/ULYSSES/ICME

Description

The list of interplanetary coronal mass ejections (CMEs) from the Ulysses spacecraft for the period from February 18, 1992, to April 5, 2008. Events from 1992 to 2002 were obtained directly from the Ulysses ICME list . Events occurring from early 2003 onward were identified by the authors (R. W. Ebert, D. J. McComas, H. A. Elliott, R. J. Forsyth, J. T. Gosling).
See publication https://doi.org/10.1029/2008JA013631
