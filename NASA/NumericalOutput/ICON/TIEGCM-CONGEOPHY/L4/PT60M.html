Description

Thermosphere Ionosphere Electrodynamics General Circulation Model (TIEGCM) L4-3 with Hough Mode Extension (HME) forcing.
The TIEGCM-CONGEOPHY output has constant geophysical drivers and the HME tides at the lower boundary. Specifically F10.7=F10.7a=71 sfu,
solar wind velocity is 400 km/s, IMF By is 0 nT, IMF Bz is 1 nT and hemispheric power HP = 12 GW. The same auroral and ion convection
models are used as for the other TIEGCM simulations. The only difference is the constant geophysical forcing to the simulation with
tidal HME at the lower boundary. These metadata include both the model input data and model output data.
