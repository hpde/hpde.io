ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalOutput/ICON/HME/LowerBoundary/L4/V1/PT24H

Description

This data product contains global diurnal and semi-diurnal tides obtained using Hough Mode Extension (HME)
for the lower boundary of TIE-GCM model. Diurnal and Semi-diurnal tides in zonal winds, meridional winds, temperature,
and geopotential heights at 97 km are provided. Latitude coverage of HME is +/- 88.75 degree.
