STEREO-A IMPACT HET Level 1 Data

Luhmann, Janet; G., Lee, Christina; von Rosenvinge, Tycho T.; Christian, Eric R. (2024). STEREO-A IMPACT HET Level 1 Data [Data set]. Space Physics Data Facility. https://doi.org/10.48322/12as-eq92 .

Description

STEREO-A In-Situ Measurements of Particles and CME Transients (IMPACT) High Energy Telescopes (HET) Level 1 1-minute data include electron fluxes in (cm^2 s sr MeV)^-1 measured in 3 energy ranges (0.7-1.4, 1.4-2.8 and 2.8-4.0 MeV) and proton fluxes in (cm^2 s sr MeV)^-1 measured in 11 energy ranges (13.6-15.1, 15.0-17.1, 17.0-19.3, 20.8-23.8, 23.8-26.4, 26.3-29.7, 29.6-33.4, 33.4-35.8, 35.6-40.5, 40-60, and 60-100 MeV).
