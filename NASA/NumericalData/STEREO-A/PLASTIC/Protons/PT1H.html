STEREO-A Plasma and Suprathermal Ion Composition (PLASTIC) 1-hour Proton Plasma Parameters

(2023). STEREO-A Plasma and Suprathermal Ion Composition (PLASTIC) 1-hour Proton Plasma Parameters [Data set]. NASA Space Physics Data Facility. https://doi.org/10.48322/nax5-0024 (CiteAs link). Accessed on .

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/STEREO-A/PLASTIC/Protons/PT1H

Description

This data product contains 1-hour averaged values of solar wind proton flow speed, density, and temperature and corresponding thermal speed. The records also hold flow velocity vector components and direction angles in HERTN and RTN coordinates. Chi-squared and statistical uncertainties are included, as are various flags. STEREO position information is given in radial distance and HEE, HEEQ and HCI direction angles.
