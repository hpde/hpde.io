Pioneer 10 Geiger Tube Telescope (GTT) 1-Hour Count Rates

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/Pioneer10/GTT/PT1H

Description

Hourly count rates from U. Iowa Geiger Tube Telescope (GTT)
carried on Pioneer 10. There are five rates: electrons > 0.06 MeV plus protons
> 25 MeV; e > 5 MeV plus p > 30 MeV; e >0.55 MeV plus p > 6.6 MeV; e > 21 MeV
plus p > 80 MeV; e > 31 MeV plus p > 80 MeV. The data are ftp-accessible in
annual ASCII files and via FTPBrowser. The hourly averages were computed at
NSSDC from 15-min data still offline (as of Feb. 2009).
