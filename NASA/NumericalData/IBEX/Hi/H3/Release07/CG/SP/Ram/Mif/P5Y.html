IBEX High Energy Neutral Atom Imager (Hi) Data Release 07, Compton-Getting corrected, Survival Probability corrected, Ram-directional, Mono Inertial Frame, Level H3 (H3), five-year average Data

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/IBEX/Hi/H3/Release07/CG/SP/Ram/MIF/P5Y

Description

The Interstellar Boundary Explorer (IBEX) has operated in space since 2008, improving understanding of the outer heliosphere and its interaction with the local interstellar medium. This data set is from Release 7 1-to-5-year-average IBEX-Hi map data for the years 2009-2014. It contains ram-directional energetic neutral atom (hydrogen) flux maps in the Mono Inertial Frame between 1 and 100 AU, with Compton-Getting correction applied to the flux spectra and survival-probability correction applied. The data consist of all-sky maps in solar ecliptic longitude and latitude for IBEX-Hi energy channels 2-6. Energy-channel FWHM ranges are 0.52-0.95, 0.84-1.55, 1.36-2.50, 1.99-3.75, and 3.13-6.00 keV, with center-point energies of 0.71, 1.11, 1.74, 2.73, and 4.29 keV, respectively. The CDAWeb ProductKey is IBEX_H3_ENA_HI_R07_CG_SP_RAM_MIF_5YR.
