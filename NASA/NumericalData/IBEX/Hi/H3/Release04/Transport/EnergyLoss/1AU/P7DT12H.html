IBEX High Energy Neutral Atom Imager (Hi) Data Release 04, H3 ENA Energy Loss or Gain Correction Tables at 1 AU, 7.5-day Cadence

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/IBEX/Hi/H3/Release04/Transport/EnergyLoss/1AU/P7DT12H

Description

The Interstellar Boundary Explorer (IBEX) High Energy Neutral Atom Imager (IBEX-Hi) Data Release 04 H3 transport products provide 7.5-day cadence tables for energetic neutral atom transport
through the heliosphere for the 2009-2012 observing interval. These products include survival probability, angular deflection, and energy loss or gain correction tables, supplied in both the spacecraft frame (SCF)
and the inertial frame at 1 AU. The tables are organized by orbit or half-orbit interval, solar ecliptic longitude or angle bin, and energy channel or energy grid, depending on the frame and product type.
The transport quantities support interpretation and correction of IBEX-Hi energetic neutral atom observations by characterizing the effects of heliospheric propagation between the termination shock and the observer.
McComas, D. J., et al. (2017), Seven Years of Imaging the Global Heliosphere with IBEX, Astrophys. J. Supp. Ser., 229(2), 41 (32 pp.),
http://doi.org/10.3847/1538-4365/aa66d8
