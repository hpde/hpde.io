IBEX Background Monitor Counts

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/IBEX/BM/Counts/PT0.24S

Description

Each record captures the counts of >~14 keV
protons arriving at the IBEX Background Monitor. Also included are
a time tag (seconds since 00:00 UT of January 6, 1980), an arrival
direction (R.A. and Dec. in J2000 celestial coordinates), and the
IBEX location. Data files are given for each 8-day orbit. The
data files are in ASCII.
