Description

The Atmospheric Waves Experiment (AWE) was launched on 9 November 2023 and installed on the exterior of the international space station on 18 November 2023. The AWE payload consists of a single instrument, the Advanced Mesospheric Temperature Mapper (AMTM), attached to the ISS in a nadir-viewing configuration. AWE investigates the connection between tropospheric weather and space weather - Visit https://www.awemission.org for more details
