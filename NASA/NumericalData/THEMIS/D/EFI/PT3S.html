Measurements on the Axial booms, 5 and 6, may have large Errors due to their Proximity to the Spacecraft as the Axial Booms are much shorter than other Booms. The Spin Plane Booms for THEMIS were not deployed until January 14, 2008 at 1700 UT. Any EFI Measurements obtained prior to this Date and Time should not be used.
