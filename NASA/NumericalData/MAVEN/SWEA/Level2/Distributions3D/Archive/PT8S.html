MAVEN SWEA Archive Rate 3D Electron Distributions Data Collection

Mitchell, David L. (2023). MAVEN SWEA Archive Rate 3D Electron Distributions Data Collection [Data set]. Planetary Data System; Space Physics Data Facility. https://doi.org/10.17189/1414221 .

ResourceID

Description

MAVEN Solar Wind Electron Analyzer (SWEA) electron energy/angle (3D) distributions in units of differential energy flux (eV/cm^2 sec ster eV) at the MAVEN archive telemetry rate. Description of the SWEA instrument can be found on the SWEA home page https://lasp.colorado.edu/maven/science/instrument-package/swea/ and in the SWEA instrument publication https://doi.org/10.1007/s11214-015-0232-1.
