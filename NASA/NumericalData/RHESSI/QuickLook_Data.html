RHESSI Quick Look FITS Data

The RHESSI Consortium: UC Berkeley (USA); NASA Goddard Space Flight Center (USA); The Paul Scherrer Institut (Switzerland); Spectrum Astro (USA) (2022). RHESSI Quick Look FITS Data [Data set]. Solar Data Analysis Center (SDAC).

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/RHESSI/QuickLook_Data

Description

FITS files of quicklook images for each flare, both full sun and flare location
Files are named hsi_type_flareid_ver.fits where type is fsimg for full sun, or qlimg for flare location, flareid is the RHESSI flare number, and ver is the version number. 

     Note: these are quicklook images, not the science-ready images in the RHESSI Image Archive in the ../rhessi_extras/imagecube_fits_v2 directory.
