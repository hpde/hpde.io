Interball Auroral Energetic Particle 2-min Key Parameters

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/Interball-2/DOK2A/KP/PT120S

Description

This data set contains 2-min key parameters of energetic particles from the Interball Auroral Probe Electron and Proton Anisotropy (DOK-2A). Electron and proton fluxes were measured witin energy ranges of 20-40 keV in fixed antisoloar direction and in scanned positions from 45 to 180 degrees to the sun direction.
