CSSWE REPTile Level 2 6-s Flux

Li, Xinlin; McGuire, Robert, E. (2023). CSSWE REPTile Level 2 6-s Flux [Data set]. Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics; Space Physics Data Facility. https://doi.org/10.48322/brd0-ak96 .

Note: Proper references, including those in BibTex or other formats , should include the "Accessed on date" as shown above to identify the version of the resource being cited in a given publication.

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/CubeSat/CSSWE/REPTile/L2/Flux/PT6S

Description

This data set contains Level 2 flux data from the Relativistic Electron and Proton Telescope integrated little experiment (REPTile)on board the Colorado Student Space Weather Experiment (CSSWE) CubeSat. Electrons and protons are measured in five energy ranges in units of differential flux (number/MeV/s/sr/cm²)including: E1: flux for electrons E= 0.5-1.63 MeV E2: flux for electrons E= 1.63-3.8 MeV P1: flux for protons P= 9-18 MeV P2: flux for protons P= 18-30 MeV P3: flux for protons P= 30-40 MeV Electrons from E3 are measured in units of integral flux (number/s/sr/cm²) for E>3.8 MeV. All flux, in units of number per 6 seconds, have been corrected for transmission and temperature noise. L is calculated using the IGRF magnetic field model. Geographic latitude, longitude and altitude (km) are provided.
