Ulysses SWOOPS Ion Parameters high resolution Data.

ResourceID
spase://NASA/NumericalData/Ulysses/SWOOPS/IonParams/CDF/PT335S

Description

This data set contains 4 to 8 minute averages of the ion density for protons and alpha particles, temperatures (min and max), and velocity data in RTN components from the Ulysses Solar Wind Observations Over the Poles of the Sun (SWOOPS) instrument. Density units are /cm3, temperature
units are K, velocity units are km/s.
