Description

This Data Set contains 3.5 hour Averages of the Solar Wind Ion Density Ratio (to O6+), Velocity And Temperature from the Ulysses Solar Wind Ion Composition Spectrometer. Data Set Contact: R. von Steiger, International Space Science Institute, Bern, Switzerland. Principal Investigators: J. Geiss, International Space Science Institute, Bern, Switzerland, and G. Gloeckler, University of Maryland, College Park, Maryland, USA. Reference: Astron. Astrophys. Suppl. Ser., 92(2), 267-289 (1992). Relevant Web Sites: http://space.umd.edu/UMD_sensors/uls_swics.html.
